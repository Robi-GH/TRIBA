T R I B A

How to play:

The game consists of an m x n grid of points, where m and n are even natural numbers. Two players take turns playing alternately. On each turn, a player selects a triangle by choosing three points from the grid that do not lie on the same straight line. The triangles must not overlap, and no two triangles can share a vertex. Triangles can, however, be nested within one another.

The game ends when a player has no valid moves remaining. The player unable to make a move loses.
